lonlat_to_vector¶

spherical_geometry.vector.lonlat_to_vector(lon, lat, degrees=True)[source]¶

Converts a location on the unit sphere from longitude and latitude to an x, y, z vector.

Parameters:

lon, lat : scalars or 1-D arrays

degrees : bool, optional

If True, (default) lon and lat are in decimal degrees, otherwise in radians.
Returns:

x, y, z : tuple of scalars or 1-D arrays of the same length
